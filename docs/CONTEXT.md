> **Author**: Pinky Collie

## Mission Context

Business Magician exists to empower deaf entrepreneurs through the complete business lifecycle, bridging the gap between Vocational Rehabilitation services and successful self-employment outcomes.

## Target Users

### Primary Users

#### 1. Deaf Entrepreneurs
- Individuals seeking to start their own business
- Existing business owners looking to grow
- Self-employed professionals needing structure

#### 2. VR Counselors
- Case managers supporting self-employment IPEs
- Specialists in deaf/hard-of-hearing services
- Employment coordinators

#### 3. VR Clients
- Individuals with approved self-employment IPEs
- Those exploring self-employment as a goal
- Participants in VR training programs

## Deaf Context Understanding

### Communication Preferences
- **ASL (American Sign Language)**: Primary language for many deaf users
- **Visual Communication**: Charts, diagrams, videos over text
- **Written English**: Secondary language consideration
- **Video Relay Services (VRS)**: For phone-based communications

### Cultural Considerations
- **Deaf Community Network**: Strong community ties and referral systems
- **Visual Business Models**: Businesses that leverage visual skills
- **Accessibility Awareness**: Deep understanding of barrier-free design
- **Bilingual/Bicultural**: Navigate both deaf and hearing worlds

### Common Business Challenges
- Communication barriers with hearing clients
- Access to traditional business resources
- Financial literacy and banking access
- Marketing to broader audiences
- Finding accessible business tools

## Vocational Rehabilitation Context

### VR Self-Employment Program
The Vocational Rehabilitation (VR) program supports individuals with disabilities in achieving employment goals, including self-employment.

### Key VR Concepts

#### Individualized Plan for Employment (IPE)
- Written plan between VR client and counselor
- Outlines employment goal and services needed
- Self-employment requires business plan approval
- Regular progress reviews and adjustments

#### Self-Employment Services
- Business plan development assistance
- Startup capital and equipment funding
- Training and skill development
- Ongoing business support services

#### VR Counselor Role
- Assess self-employment feasibility
- Approve and monitor IPE goals
- Coordinate services and funding
- Provide guidance and resources

## ADA Compliance Context

### Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA)
Business Magician ensures all recommendations comply with ADA requirements.

### Key ADA Considerations

#### Title I - Employment
- Reasonable accommodations in the workplace
- Non-discrimination in hiring practices
- Accessible job applications and interviews

#### Title III - Public Accommodations
- Accessible business premises
- Effective communication requirements
- Service animal policies

### Accessibility Best Practices
- Video captioning for all content
- ASL interpretation availability
- Written communication alternatives
- Accessible digital platforms
- Physical accessibility planning

## Business Lifecycle Context

### Phase 1: IDEA
**Goal**: Validate and refine business concept

**Key Activities**:
- Skills and interest assessment
- Market research and analysis
- Feasibility study development
- VR counselor consultation
- Initial concept documentation

**VR Integration**:
- Feasibility study for IPE
- Self-employment goal discussion
- Skills assessment alignment

### Phase 2: BUILD
**Goal**: Establish legal business entity

**Key Activities**:
- Entity type selection
- State registration process
- Federal EIN acquisition
- Business banking setup
- License and permit acquisition
- Insurance requirements

**VR Integration**:
- Business plan approval
- Startup funding requests
- Equipment purchase coordination

### Phase 3: GROW
**Goal**: Expand business operations

**Key Activities**:
- Marketing strategy execution
- Customer acquisition
- Revenue growth tracking
- Team building (if applicable)
- Partnership development

**VR Integration**:
- Progress reviews
- Additional service requests
- Success milestone documentation

### Phase 4: MANAGED
**Goal**: Maintain sustainable operations

**Key Activities**:
- Compliance monitoring
- Financial management
- Annual renewals
- Strategic planning
- Exit planning (if applicable)

**VR Integration**:
- Case closure preparation
- Long-term success tracking
- Alumni network participation
