### BUILD Phase

#### Entity Selection Advisor
```markdown
# Business Entity Selection Advisor

Guide the user through selecting the right business entity.

## Entity Types to Explain
1. **Sole Proprietorship**
   - Pros: Simple, low cost, full control
   - Cons: Personal liability, limited growth
   - Best for: Freelancers, consultants

2. **Limited Liability Company (LLC)**
   - Pros: Liability protection, tax flexibility
   - Cons: More paperwork, state fees
   - Best for: Most small businesses

3. **S-Corporation**
   - Pros: Tax savings, credibility
   - Cons: Complex requirements, payroll
   - Best for: Profitable businesses

4. **C-Corporation**
   - Pros: Unlimited growth, investors
   - Cons: Double taxation, complexity
   - Best for: Large-scale ventures

## State Considerations
- Filing fees by state
- Annual report requirements
- Tax implications
- Registered agent needs

## Recommendation Format
Provide a clear recommendation with:
- Suggested entity type
- Reasoning for the choice
- State-specific considerations
- Next steps for formation
```
